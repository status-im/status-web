If you are a group chat owner and you no longer need the [group chat](./understand-group-chats.mdx), you can [remove all members](./remove-members-from-a-group-chat.mdx) from the group chat and then [leave the group chat](./leave-a-group-chat.mdx).

<Admonition type="info">
  You can't delete a group chat directly.
</Admonition>
