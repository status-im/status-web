You can format text inside your messages to clarify or emphasize something. Formatting can make your messages easier to read, especially if they're long.

In Status, you format your messages using the formatting toolbar or markup by adding special characters.

## Formatting toolbar

To format your text using the formatting toolbar, select the text and choose a formatting option. To format a message you've already sent, [edit the message](./edit-and-delete-messages.mdx). The formatting toolbar is only available on Status desktop.

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dhgck7ebz/image/upload/v1694416770/Help/Documentation%20Screens/Messaging/275/275_image_01_light.png)

<Admonition type="tip">
  When you press <ContextTag>Enter</ContextTag> on your keyboard in Status desktop, you're sending your message. To start a new line, press <ContextTag>Shift+Enter</ContextTag>.
</Admonition>
