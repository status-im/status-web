<Admonition type="beta" status="Status app is currently in beta stage">
  The Status app is currently in its beta stage. This means the application is still undergoing active development, and certain features described in this document may function differently or be unavailable within the app.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="info">
  Currently, the profile sections you can view or edit on Status mobile and Status desktop are different.
</Admonition>

Your profile serves as a showcase of who you are to other Status users. You can make as many edits as you need, and the profile updates take effect instantly.

<Admonition type="info">
  Your Status profile and messages are temporarily saved in the [Waku](https://waku.org)<ExternalIcon /> peer-to-peer network and stored permanently on your device. They are not saved on the blockchain or transmitted via the Ethereum network.
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="tip">
  To see how your profile appears to others, check out [View your profile as someone else](./view-your-profile-as-someone-else.mdx).
</Admonition>

### Profile picture

You can take a photo or use an image as your profile picture.

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<CameraIcon />}>Camera</ContextTag> in your profile picture.
    1. In the action sheet, choose <ContextTag icon={<CameraIcon />}>Take photo</ContextTag> or <ContextTag icon={<ImageIcon />}>Select from gallery</ContextTag>.
    1. Authorize the Status app to access your camera or photo gallery.
    1. Set a profile picture and tap <ContextTag>Choose</ContextTag> or <ContextTag icon={<CheckIcon />}>Confirm</ContextTag>, depending your phone model.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. Click <ContextTag icon={<Edit2DesktopIcon />}>Edit</ContextTag> in your profile picture.
    1. Click <ContextTag icon={<ImageDesktopIcon />}>Select different image</ContextTag> if there is an existing profile photo.
    1. Choose a picture from your computer.
    1. Adjust your picture size and click <ContextTag>Make this my profile picture</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

### Name, bio or accent colour

Use Status mobile if you want to edit the accent colour.

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. Under <ContextTag>Profile</ContextTag>, choose the item you want to edit.
    1. Edit the content and click <ContextTag>Save</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. Update content in the <ContextTag>Display name</ContextTag> or <ContextTag>Bio</ContextTag> field.
    1. Click <ContextTag>Save changes</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

<Admonition type="info">
  If you leave your display name empty, Status assigns a random three-word name to your profile.
</Admonition>

### Communities, accounts or collectibles

Such information is hidden by default. You can choose what to reveal to different groups of Status users.

<Tabs defaultValue="Desktop">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile" disabled>
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. In the <ContextTag>Showcase</ContextTag> list, choose <ContextTag>Communities</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>Accounts</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>Collectibles</ContextTag> or <ContextTag>Assets</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap the item you want to edit.
    1. Choose <ContextTag>Everyone</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>Contacts</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>ID verified contacts</ContextTag> or <ContextTag>No one</ContextTag>.

    <Admonition type="info">
      ID verified contacts are Status contacts whose identity you have confirmed by [sending them a verification request with a question](./verify-your-contacts-identity.mdx).
    </Admonition>
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. Choose the <ContextTag>Communities</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>Accounts</ContextTag> or <ContextTag>Collectibles</ContextTag> tab.
    1. On the right of the item you want to edit, click the drop-down menu.
    1. Select <ContextTag icon={<LanguageDesktopIcon />}>Everyone</ContextTag>, <ContextTag icon={<ProfileDesktopIcon />}>Contacts</ContextTag>, or <ContextTag icon={<HideDesktopIcon />}>No one</ContextTag>.
    1. Click <ContextTag>Save changes</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

### Web links

If you want to tell people more about yourself, you can display social media links and other web links on your profile.

<Tabs defaultValue="Desktop">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile" disabled>
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. In the <ContextTag>On the web</ContextTag> list, tap <ContextTag>Links</ContextTag>.
    1. Choose the action you want to complete and follow the steps from this table:

           <Table>
             <TableHead>
               <TableHeader>Action</TableHeader>
               <TableHeader>Steps</TableHeader>
             </TableHead>

             <TableContent>
               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Add links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<AddSmallIcon />}>Add</ContextTag> and select the type of link to add.
                   1. If you want to add a website, type the name and URL. If you want to add your social media, type your username.
                   1. Tap <ContextTag>Save</ContextTag>.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Reorder links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   In the <ContextTag>Links</ContextTag> list, hold and drag the item to the position you want.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Edit links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   1. Tap the link you want to edit.
                   1. Update the content and tap <ContextTag>Save</ContextTag>.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Delete links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   1. Swipe left over the link to delete and tap <ContextTag icon={<DeleteIcon />}>Delete</ContextTag>.
                   1. Tap <ContextTag>Delete</ContextTag> again to confirm.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>
             </TableContent>
           </Table>
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. Choose the action you want to complete.
    1. Follow the steps from this table:

           <Table>
             <TableHead>
               <TableHeader>Action</TableHeader>
               <TableHeader>Steps</TableHeader>
             </TableHead>

             <TableContent>
               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Add links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   1. Scroll down and click <ContextTag>+ Add a link</ContextTag>.
                   1. Select the type of link to add.
                   1. If you want to add a website, type the URL. If you want to add your social media, type your username.
                   1. Click <ContextTag>Add</ContextTag>, then click <ContextTag>Save changes</ContextTag>.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Reorder links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   1. Scroll down and find the link to reorder.
                   1. Hold and drag the item to the position you want.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Edit links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   1. Scroll down and find the link to edit.
                   1. Click <ContextTag icon={<Edit2DesktopIcon />}>Edit</ContextTag>.
                   1. Type the new content and click <ContextTag>Update</ContextTag>.
                   1. Click <ContextTag>Save changes</ContextTag>.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Delete links</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   1. Scroll down and find the link to delete.
                   1. Click <ContextTag icon={<Edit2DesktopIcon />}>Edit</ContextTag>.
                   1. Click <ContextTag>Delete</ContextTag>, then click <ContextTag>Save changes</ContextTag>.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>
             </TableContent>
           </Table>
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>
