  The Status app is currently in its beta stage. This means the application is still undergoing active development, and certain features described in this document may function differently or be unavailable within the app.
</Admonition>

Nicknames are familiar or custom names you can assign to people you interact with in Status. Nicknames offer a practical solution for remembering names that may be difficult to recall or distinguish.

<Admonition type="info">
  Nicknames you add to others are personal and only visible to you.
</Admonition>

When you assign a nickname to someone, the Status app prioritizes displaying the nickname over the original name chosen by the contact. The nickname and the contact's original name are always visible.

<Admonition type="tip">
  You can give nicknames to anyone in Status, not only your contacts. You can't add a nickname to yourself.
</Admonition>

## Add a nickname to someone's profile

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. Open the chat, group chat or channel with the person you want to add a nickname to.
    1. Tap the person's name you want to add a nickname.
    1. From the top navigation, tap <ContextTag icon={<OptionsIcon />}>More options</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<ProfileIcon />}>View profile</ContextTag>.
    1. From the top navigation, tap <ContextTag icon={<OptionsIcon />}>More options</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag icon={<EditIcon />}>Add nickname</ContextTag>.
    1. Enter your desired nickname and tap <ContextTag>Add nickname</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. Open the chat, group chat or channel with the person you want to add a nickname to.
    1. Click the person's name and select <ContextTag icon={<OptionsIcon />}>More options</ContextTag>.
    1. Click <ContextTag icon={<ProfileDesktopIcon />}>View profile</ContextTag>.
    1. Click <ContextTag icon={<OptionsIcon />}>More options</ContextTag>.
    1. Click <ContextTag icon={<Edit2DesktopIcon />}>Add nickname</ContextTag>.
    1. In the <ContextTag>Add nickname</ContextTag> pop-up window, enter your desired nickname and click <ContextTag>Add nickname</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

## Remove a nickname from someone's profile

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
