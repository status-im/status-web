You can turn off and on your notifications for the entire app or specific communities, channels, group chats or direct message (DM) chats for a designated period. Communities, group chats, channels and DM chats appear greyed out when you mute their notifications.

<Admonition type="tip">
  You have the option to [set notification exceptions](./set-up-and-customize-notification-exceptions.mdx) to fine-tune your notification preferences, allowing certain notifications to come through while others are muted. For instance, you can mute all notifications for a channel except for messages sent by a specific user.
</Admonition>

## Mute all the Status app notifications

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap your profile picture.
    1. Scroll down in the list and tap <ContextTag icon={<NotificationsIcon />}>Notifications</ContextTag>.
    1. Turn <ContextTag>Notifications</ContextTag> off.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag>.
    1. In the <ContextTag>Settings</ContextTag> sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Notifications and Sounds</ContextTag>.
    1. Turn <ContextTag>Notifications</ContextTag> off.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

<Admonition type="info">
  Muting all the Status app notifications mutes ephemeral notifications as well (the ones appearing on your screen and disappearing shortly after).
</Admonition>

## Mute specific notifications

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<CommunitiesIcon />}>Communities</ContextTag> to mute a community or channel, or tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag> to mute a group chat or DM chat.
    1. Tap the community, channel, group chat or DM chat you want to mute and, in the top navigation, tap <ContextTag icon={<OptionsIcon />}>More options</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<MutedIcon />}>Mute</ContextTag>.
    1. Select the length of time you'd like to mute your notifications.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    ### Mute communities

    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag>.
    1. From the settings sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<CommunitiesDesktopIcon />}>Communities</ContextTag>.
    1. Choose the community you want to mute, and click <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Mute</ContextTag>.

    ### Mute channels

    1. From the navigation sidebar, click the community with the channel you wish to mute.
    1. In the channel sidebar, right-click the channel.
    1. Select <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Mute</ContextTag>.

    ### Mute group chats or DMs

    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<ChatDesktopIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. In the <ContextTag>Messages</ContextTag> sidebar, right-click the group chat or DM chat you wish to mute.
    1. Select <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Mute</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

<Admonition type="info">
  You still see a badge if someone mentions you in a muted channel, community, group chat, or direct message. This badge displays the number of mentions.
</Admonition>
