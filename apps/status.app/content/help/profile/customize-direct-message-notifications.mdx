By default, you receive notifications for direct messages (DMs). You can customize the DM chat notifications and receive only the ones relevant to you.

In addition to DM chat notifications, you also receive notifications for mentions. For example, if you mute someone, they can still mention you in a community channel or group chat. To customize your mention notifications, read [Customize mentions and other messages](./customize-mentions-and-other-messages-notifications.mdx).

## Mute direct message notifications from a user

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap the DM chat you want to mute and, in the top navigation, tap <ContextTag icon={<OptionsIcon />}>More options</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<MutedIcon />}>Mute</ContextTag>.
    1. Select the amount of time you'd like to mute your notifications.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<ChatDesktopIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Under the messages list, right-click the DM chat you want to mute.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Mute chat</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

<Admonition type="tip">
  When you mute notifications for a DM, it gets greyed out and <ContextTag icon={<MutedIcon />}>Mute</ContextTag> appears next to the user's name.
</Admonition>

## Mute all direct message notifications

This action mutes notifications from all your direct messages (DMs). If you want to mute all the Status app notifications, read [Mute your notifications](./mute-your-notifications.mdx).

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap your profile picture.
    1. Scroll down the list and tap <ContextTag icon={<NotificationsIcon />}>Notifications</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Direct messages</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Your default notification settings</ContextTag>.
    1. Turn off <ContextTag>All messages</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag>.
    1. In the **Settings** sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Notifications and Sounds</ContextTag>.
    1. Turn <ContextTag>Notifications</ContextTag> off.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

## Customize direct message notifications

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap the DM chat you want to set up notifications for and, in the top navigation, tap <ContextTag icon={<OptionsIcon />}>More options</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<NotificationsIcon />}>Notification settings</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Personal mentions</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>Global mentions</ContextTag> or <ContextTag>All other messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Choose your notification preference.

           <Table>
             <TableHead>
               <TableHeader>Preference </TableHeader>
               <TableHeader>Description</TableHeader>
             </TableHead>

             <TableContent>
               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Notifications on</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   Turn this on if you want to receive notifications banners and lock screen notifications for direct message notifications.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>In notification centre only</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   Turn this on to receive direct message notifications in the Status notification centre.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Notifications off</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   Turn this off if you don't want to receive any direct message notifications.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>
             </TableContent>
           </Table>
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag>.
    1. From the <ContextTag>Settings sidebar</ContextTag>, click <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Notifications and Sounds</ContextTag>.
    1. Under the <ContextTag>Messages</ContextTag> area, click on the drop-down menu next to <ContextTag>1:1 Chats</ContextTag>.
    1. Choose your notification preference.

           <Table>
             <TableHead>
               <TableHeader>Preference </TableHeader>
               <TableHeader>Description</TableHeader>
             </TableHead>

             <TableContent>
               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Send alerts</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   Turn this on if you want to see notifications.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Deliver quietly</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   Turn this on to receive subtle notifications without banners. You need to unlock your phone to see your notifications.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>

               <TableRow>
                 <TableCell>Turn off</TableCell>

                 <TableCell>
                   Turn this off if you don't want to receive notifications.
                 </TableCell>
               </TableRow>
             </TableContent>
           </Table>
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>
