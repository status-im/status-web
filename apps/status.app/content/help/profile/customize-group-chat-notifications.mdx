You can choose which message and mention notifications you want to receive for any group chat. This allows to display only the notifications that are relevant to you.

## Mute notifications in specific group chats

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Long press the group chat you want to mute.
    1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<MutedIcon />}>Mute</ContextTag>.
    1. Select the length of time you'd like to mute your notifications.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<ChatDesktopIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Under the messages list, right-click the group you want to mute.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Mute chat</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

<Admonition type="tip">
  When you mute notifications for a group chat, it appears greyed with a <MutedIcon />Muted icon.
</Admonition>

## Mute group chat notifications except personal mentions

To avoid missing important messages, you can mute some notifications. Here's how to mute all group chat notifications except personal mentions.

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Long press the group chat you want to mute.
    1. Tap <ContextTag icon={<NotificationsIcon />}>Notifications</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Global mentions</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Notifications off</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag>All other messages</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Notifications off</ContextTag>.
    1. Verify <ContextTag>Personal mentions</ContextTag> is set to <ContextTag>On</ContextTag>. If it is not, select <ContextTag>Personal mentions</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Notifications on</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag>.
    1. From the <ContextTag>Settings sidebar</ContextTag>, click <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Notifications and Sounds</ContextTag>.
    1. Scroll down to the <ContextTag>Exceptions</ContextTag> area and click <ContextTag icon={<AddDesktopIcon />}>Add</ContextTag> next to the group chat you want to mute.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Global mentions</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Turn off</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag>All other messages</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Turn off</ContextTag>.
    1. In the same pop-up menu, make sure <ContextTag>Personal mentions</ContextTag> is set to <ContextTag>Send alerts</ContextTag>. If it's not, then select <ContextTag>Personal mentions</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Send Alerts</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

## Mute all group chats notifications

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<MessengerIcon />}>Messages</ContextTag>.
    1. Tap your profile picture.
    1. Scroll down the list of settings and tap <ContextTag icon={<ActivityCenterIcon />}>Notifications</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Group chats</ContextTag> > <ContextTag>Your default notification settings</ContextTag>.
    1. Turn off <ContextTag>Personal mentions</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>Global mentions</ContextTag> and <ContextTag>All other messages</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag>.
    1. From the<ContextTag>Settings sidebar</ContextTag>, click <ContextTag icon={<NotificationDesktopIcon />}>Notifications and Sounds</ContextTag>.
    1. Under the<ContextTag>Messages</ContextTag> area, click on the drop-down menu next to<ContextTag>Group chats</ContextTag>.
    1. Select <ContextTag>Turn off</ContextTag>.

    <Admonition type="info">
      For desktop, you mute a specific group chat by creating an exception.
    </Admonition>
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>
