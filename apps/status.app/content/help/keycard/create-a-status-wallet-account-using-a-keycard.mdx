When you create a [Status Wallet](../wallet/status-wallet-your-quick-start-guide.mdx) account with a Keycard, you generate and store the account directly on the Keycard instead of your device, ensuring it never connects to the internet.

You can [create multiple Wallet accounts on a new Keycard](#create-a-wallet-account-using-an-empty-keycard), or start with one and [add additional accounts](#add-a-wallet-account-to-a-used-keycard) later. If you don't have a Keycard, you can [order one](https://status.app/keycard)<ExternalIcon />.

## What to expect

- You need a [card reader](./keycard-faq.mdx) to connect a Keycard to your computer.
- Once you create a Wallet account using a Keycard, you must use the Keycard to confirm transactions for this account.
- If you want to generate a new [recovery phrase](../profile/understand-your-status-keys-and-recovery-phrase.mdx#about-your-recovery-phrase) for the new Wallet account, you need an empty Keycard. If your Keycard is not new, [view the wallet accounts on your Keycard](./view-the-profile-or-wallet-accounts-on-your-keycard.mdx) and [factory reset](./factory-reset-your-keycard.mdx) it when needed.
- A single Keycard can hold multiple Wallet accounts that use the same recovery phrase.

## Create a Wallet account using an empty Keycard

When creating a Wallet account using an empty Keycard, you generate a new recovery phrase for the account.

    1. Open the Status app and plug in your Keycard using a card reader. Allow the connection if you see a security alert from your computer.
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag> > <ContextTag icon={<KeycardDesktopIcon />}>Keycard</ContextTag>, then click <ContextTag>Create a new Keycard account with a new seed phrase</ContextTag>.
    1. Create a six-digit PIN for your Keycard, and repeat the PIN to confirm. Then click <ContextTag>Next</ContextTag>.
    1. Click <ContextTag>Reveal seed phrase</ContextTag> when you are sure no one else can see it.
    1. Carefully write down the 12-word recovery phrase displayed on the screen. The Status app or your Keycard doesn't save this information, and you can't see it again. After you've done, click <ContextTag>Next</ContextTag> to continue.
    1. Confirm your recovery phrase by entering the words as required. Then click <ContextTag>Next</ContextTag>.

## Add a Wallet account to a used Keycard

You can create new Wallet accounts as needed on a used Keycard. All Wallet accounts use the same recovery phrase.

<Tabs defaultValue="Desktop">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile" disabled>
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. Open the Status app and plug in your Keycard using a card reader. Allow the connection if you see a security alert from your computer.
    1. From the tab bar, tap <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag> > <ContextTag icon={<WalletDesktopIcon />}>Wallet</ContextTag>, then click <ContextTag>Add new account</ContextTag>.
    1. Name your account, select a colour, and pick an emoji by clicking the default one.
    1. Click your profile picture under <ContextTag>Origin</ContextTag> and choose the Keycard to use.
    1. Click <ContextTag>Add account</ContextTag>.
    1. Use your Keycard PIN or biometrics to confirm.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>
