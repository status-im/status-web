title: Cancel transactions
author: earakel, jorge-campo

Once your transaction is confirmed on the blockchain, it's impossible to cancel it, no matter which wallet app you're using. All blockchain transactions are always irreversible. For more details, see [Understand transaction confirmations and statuses](./understand-transaction-confirmations-and-statuses.mdx).
