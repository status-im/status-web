<Admonition type="info">
  You can only customize channels using Status desktop.

As a [Status Community Owner](./how-to-run-a-status-community-your-quick-start-guide.mdx), you can [create channels](./create-a-channel.mdx) in your community, [organize channels into categories](./organize-channels-using-categories.mdx) and customize these channels and categories as you need.

<Admonition type="info">
  Read [Delete a channel from your community](./delete-a-channel.mdx) if you want to delete channels.
</Admonition>

## Customize your channel

<Tabs defaultValue="Desktop">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile" disabled>
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click your community.
    1. Right-click the channel, then click <ContextTag icon={<Edit1DesktopIcon />}>Edit channel</ContextTag>.
    1. You can change the <ContextTag>name</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>colour</ContextTag>, <ContextTag>emoji</ContextTag> and <ContextTag>description</ContextTag> for your community channel. The <ContextTag>name</ContextTag> and <ContextTag>description</ContextTag> are mandatory fields.
    1. Optionally, check the box to <ContextTag>Hide channel from members who don't have</ContextTag> <ContextTag>permissions to view the channel</ContextTag>.
    1. Optionally, click <ContextTag>Add permission</ContextTag> to add channel permissions or click <ContextTag icon={<Edit2DesktopIcon />}>Edit</ContextTag> to update an existing permission. Turn on or off the toggle to choose if <ContextTag>Users with view-only permissions can add reactions</ContextTag>.
    1. Click <ContextTag>Save changes</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

<Admonition type="info">
  For details about setting up permissions, read [Set up channel permissions](./set-up-your-channel-permissions.mdx).
</Admonition>

## Reorder channels

You can reorder channels in your community to make the important ones more accessible.

<Tabs defaultValue="Desktop">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile" disabled>
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click your community.
    1. Click and hold the channel you want to reorder.
    1. Drag the channel to where you want it to be.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>

<Admonition type="info">
  Read [Delete a channel from your community](./delete-a-channel.mdx) if you want to delete channels.
</Admonition>
