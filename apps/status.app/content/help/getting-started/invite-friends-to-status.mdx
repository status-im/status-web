title: Invite friends to Status
author: earakel
---


If some of your friends don't have Status profiles yet, you can invite them and help them find you in the app. Once they [download](https://status.app) and install Status, [share your profile with them](../profile/share-your-status-profile.mdx) and ask them to [add you to their contacts](../messaging/add-a-contact-in-status.mdx).
