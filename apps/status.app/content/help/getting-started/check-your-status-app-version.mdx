You can check the version of the Status app you're running to ensure it's up to date or for troubleshooting purposes.

## Check your Status app version

<Tabs defaultValue="Mobile">
  <TabsList size={32}>
    <TabsTrigger value="Mobile">
      Mobile
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Desktop">
      Desktop
    </TabsTrigger>

    <TabsTrigger value="Web" disabled>
      Web
    </TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>

  <TabsContent value="Mobile">
    1. Open the Status app on your mobile device.
    1. Tap your profile picture, and then scroll down and tap <ContextTag>About</ContextTag>.
    1. The version of the Status app you're running is displayed next to <ContextTag>App version</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>

  <TabsContent value="Desktop">
    1. Open the Status app on your desktop.
    1. From the navigation sidebar, click <ContextTag icon={<SettingsDesktopIcon />}>Settings</ContextTag> > <ContextTag icon={<InfoDesktopIcon />}>About</ContextTag>.
    1. The version of the Status app you're running is displayed above <ContextTag>Current version</ContextTag>.
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>
