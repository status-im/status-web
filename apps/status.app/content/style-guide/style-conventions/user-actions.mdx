language: en
title: User actions
---

When you describe a UI interaction, use these action verbs:

<Table>
  <TableHead>
    <TableHeader>Action</TableHeader>
    <TableHeader>Style convention (desktop, web and mobile)</TableHeader>
  </TableHead>
  <TableContent>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Go to a menu or screen</TableCell>
      <TableCell>Use *go to*.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Click a URL</TableCell>
      <TableCell>For URLs, the verb depends on whether you're offering extra information or redirecting the user to another article that might be a better fit for their needs. If you're linking an article with additional information the user might want to know, use *check out*. If you're suggesting they might want to close the article they're currently reading and take a look at a more relevant one, use *see [this] instead*.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Selecting a single UI element</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*Click* (desktop and web) or *tap* (mobile).</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Selecting one or more options from a list</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*Choose*. Example: *Choose a name and highlight colour for your group chat*.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Selecting specific options from a list or selecting text</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*Select*. Example: *Select any channel with a lock icon from the channel list*. </TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Checkboxes</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*Check* the [UI label] box (to enable) / *uncheck* the [UI label] box (to disable).</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Switches</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*turn on* / *turn off*.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Going to previous screen</TableCell>
      <TableCell>Use *return*.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Swiping element</TableCell>
      <TableCell>Use *swipe* [left or right] for desktop, web, and mobile.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Long pressing</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*Long press* for mobile interactions, when required.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Right clicking</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*Right click* for desktop and web interactions, when required.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Starting session</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*log in* (verb) or *login* (noun) for desktop, web, and mobile. *Log in to*, not *log into*.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>Ending session</TableCell>
      <TableCell>*log out* (verb) or *logout* (noun) for desktop, web, and mobile.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
    <TableRow>
      <TableCell>First-time access</TableCell>
      <TableCell>Use *create profile*.</TableCell>
    </TableRow>
  </TableContent>
</Table>
