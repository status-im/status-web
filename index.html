Messenger

Chat privately

Built for free speech. Peer-to-peer, fully end-to-end encrypted messaging with no backdoors, designed exclusively for secure and confidential communication with friends, family, and business partners, so no one can read or trace your conversations

No identity required to sign up

Sign up with nothing at all. No phone number, email, card, social account, bank account, or personal ID required to create a Status profile. Your profile is an anonymous identity created on your device from the moment you start, and it belongs to you alone, so you are in control of your digital identity from day one

Keep private who your friends are

Other apps record what you say while still logging who you talk to and when. Status records nothing. Your contacts, timing, and connections stay invisible to everyone, including the network itself

Communities

Self-sovereign communities

Group chats, forums, and communities built for real people. Create independent, censorship-resistant spaces, set your own rules, and moderate locally, without a corporation deciding what is allowed. Ownership stays in the hands of communities

Unified Team Workspace

Ditch the clutter of scattered chats, links, and browser tabs. Status Desktop brings your entire workspace into a single, high-performance local app. Coordinate with your team and access key information without losing focus
