28:T527a,<p><a href="https://orvex.fi/">Orvex</a> is the native <a href="https://status.network/blog/dex-vs-cex-which-is-better-for-trading-in-2026">decentralized exchange (DEX)</a> built on <a href="https://status.network/">Status Network</a>, a fully gasless Ethereum Layer 2 built on reputation, composable privacy, and network-level spam protection. Rather than charging users gas fees per swap, Orvex executes trades gaslessly by leveraging Status Network’s native yield infrastructure and reputation-based access control through <a href="https://docs.status.network/tokenomics/karmic-tokenomics">Karma</a>, a <a href="https://status.network/blog/what-is-a-soulbound-token-non-transferable-reputation-explained">soulbound non-transferable reputation token</a>.</p><p>Liquidity providers on Orvex earn from three simultaneous income streams:</p><ul><li>Native staking or lending yield from the underlying bridged assets</li><li>Traditional DEX swap fees (LP commissions)</li><li>Additional Karma rewards from Status Network’s governance system</li></ul><h2 id="how-zero-fee-trading-works-on-orvex">How Zero-Fee Trading Works on Orvex</h2><h3 id="gasless-execution-via-rln">Gasless Execution via RLN</h3><p>Orvex trades are gasless because Status Network uses Rate Limiting Nullifiers (RLN) to prevent spam instead of gas fees. RLN is a zero-knowledge cryptographic protocol that:</p><ol><li>Assigns each user a fair share of free transactions based on their Karma balance</li><li>Proves transaction validity without revealing the user’s identity (privacy via cryptographic commitments)</li><li>Enforces rate limits using Sparse Merkle Trees (height 20, supporting up to 1 million accounts)</li><li>Uses Shamir Secret Sharing and ZKPs to ensure no single entity controls the denial-of-service threshold</li></ol><p>When a user submits a swap on Orvex, the transaction is routed through Status Network’s sequencer, which validates the RLN proof and includes the trade in the next batch. No gas payment is required.</p><h3 id="revenue-model">Revenue Model</h3><p>Orvex’s revenue model is part of Status Network’s broader yield-backed economic model. For a full explanation of how revenue sources, the Apps Funding Pool, and zero gas work together, see <a href="https://status.network/blog/status-network-economic-model-yield-funding-pool-and-zero-gas">Status Network Economic Model: Revenue Sources, Funding Pool, and Zero Gas</a>.</p><p>In the context of Orvex specifically:</p><ul><li><strong>Native DEX swap fees</strong>: a percentage of swap volume flows into the Apps Funding Pool and network operations.</li><li><strong>Karma-governed pair funding</strong>: Karma holders vote to direct funding pool allocations to specific liquidity pairs on Orvex, boosting incentives for high-priority pools.</li><li><strong>Premium gas option</strong>: users exceeding their gasless quota can pay a premium fee to execute additional transactions, split between Status Network and the RLN protocol.</li></ul><h3 id="karma-based-transaction-tiers">Karma-Based Transaction Tiers</h3><p>Access to Orvex’s zero-fee trading is tiered by Karma, a <a href="https://status.network/blog/what-is-a-soulbound-token-non-transferable-reputation-explained">non-transferable soulbound token</a> representing reputation and contribution to Status Network. Each pool is anchored in a yield-bearing asset. Liquidity providers earn three income streams simultaneously: native yield from the underlying asset, traditional LP swap fees, and Karma rewards voted on by governance.</p><p>Karma holders vote on which pools receive additional incentive allocations from the <strong>Apps Funding Pool</strong>. This means the community actively directs yield toward pairs with the most strategic value for the ecosystem.</p><h3 id="privacy-via-bermuda">Privacy via Bermuda</h3><p>Orvex integrates Bermuda, Status Network’s native composable privacy layer, enabling:</p><ul><li>Confidential balance inquiries, users see their holdings without broadcasting to the network</li><li>Stealth accounts, trades linked to multiple addresses for unlinkability</li><li>Private swap execution, transaction amounts and endpoints obscured</li></ul><p>Privacy is optional; traders can choose public execution. The option exists natively, making Orvex unique among DEXs in offering privacy-by-design rather than as a bolt-on.</p><h3 id="liquidity-incentive-governance">Liquidity Incentive Governance</h3><p>Karma holders vote on DEX gauge incentives, allocating emissions and additional rewards to specific Orvex pools. This means the community, not a core team, decides which pairs attract the most capital. Incentive allocation evolves as the ecosystem grows, creating a governance-driven market design.</p><h2 id="why-orvex-matters-in-the-status-network-ecosystem">Why Orvex Matters in the Status Network Ecosystem</h2><p>Orvex is not just a trading venue. It is a core component of Status Network’s economic engine:</p><ol><li><strong>Revenue generation</strong>: swap fees from Orvex flow directly into the Apps Funding Pool, providing continuous capital to builders and liquidity providers.</li><li><strong>Flywheel effect</strong>: as Orvex volume grows, more fees accumulate.
